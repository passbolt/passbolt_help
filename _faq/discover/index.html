--- title: Discover passbolt layout: faq category: discover notsearchable: true slug: faq --- Passbolt is a free and open source password manager that allows team members to store and share credentials securely. For instance, the wifi password of your office, the administrator password of a router or your organisation social media account password, all of them can be secured using passbolt.
TL;DR
  • Free & Open source
  • Designed for teams
  • Extensible API
